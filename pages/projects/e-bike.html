EE453 E-Bike Power Electronics System Design

For this project, my partner and I designed the power electronics hardware and controls to convert power from a 24V battery to an AC waveform that controls the BLDC motor of an E-bike.

The process of designing and building this system included creating electrical schematics, fabricating a PCB, developing digital signal processor controls, and testing the integrated control system with a resistive load.

Our boost converter was able to successfully boost the 24V input voltage to 48V using a PI control with an inner current loop and an outer voltage loop, which was then fed to a 3-phase H-bridge inverter tested with open loop control. Waveforms of this testing can be seen below.
