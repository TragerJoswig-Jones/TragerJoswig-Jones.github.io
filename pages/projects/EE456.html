Sustainable Power Systems Capstone Projects

A collection of hypothetical power system design projects for the EE 456, Computer-Aided Design in Power System, capstone course.

  • Design of a collector system for a hypothetical 120 MW wind farm by considering routing, protection, and relevant standards to create an economical design that balanced capital costs, reliability, and losses.
  • A 7-Year transmission reinforcement plan for a hypothetical utility company to economically meet an N-1 contingency case criteria with the addition of a 120 MW wind farm and projected load growth in the system.
  • A plan for the expansion of the Total Transfer Capacity of the interconnection of power system networks in a modified Western United State power system in order to reduce congestion costs and maintenance system stability.
